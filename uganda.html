Uganda

Known as the Pearl of Africa, Uganda’s many tribes comprise much of its population, and the country is known for its warmth and hospitality. It is home to the largest freshwater lake in Africa and it hosts the largest number of primates on the continent too, with the highest number of mountain gorillas worldwide. On the edge of the landscaped Harare Gardens, the National Gallery of Zimbabwe—located in the country’s capital—also hosts a large collection of African contemporary art and traditional pieces like baskets, textiles, jewelry and musical instruments.
