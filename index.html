Vision

To become a one stop hub of information, knowledge, business and Investment opportunities for East Africa & the rest of Africa

Mission

Shape the supply chain between Africa & the rest of the world and become No 1 connector of markets & consumers of services and products in all business sectors such as Agriculture, Tourism, Construction, Mining, Manufacturing and more between East Africa and the rest of the world (Europe, USA, Asia)
