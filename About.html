About Us

  • About Us

We are a one stop knowledge hub for Tanzania and the rest of Africa.

The Tanzania Chamber of Commerce Ireland, Europe (TCCIE) was established to connect Tanzania/Africa with Europe and the rest of the world

We primarily champion trade between Tanzania/Africa and Europe. Our goal is to move to a higher level on the international trade map. We play a vital role in promoting the business interests of our members in Europe, Africa and globally .

We are a member of the European Federation of Bilateral Chambers of Commerce, This affords us an opportunity to engage with other chambers with a common purpose in a meaningful dialogue and expand our International business network.

We provide value to our members through ample virtual and face to face networking events and educational forums.. We have a good partnership with local Irish, other EU and African Chambers and professional networks

Excellent virtual and face to face networking events help our members to find new markets, new business partners, and investors.

Vital role

The Chamber plays a vital role in facilitating business meetings between foreign Investors and Tanzanian/African Governments and the communities where it serves.

  • Global business thinking

    • Africa - the world’s next big growth market

    Africa’s potential as a growth market for business remains both underestimated and misunderstood. How many companies in Africa earn annual revenues of $1 billion or more? Many would say very few or zero . The reality? More than 400 such companies exist—and they are, on average, both faster growing and more profitable than their global peers.

    Africa’s fast-growing population and markets present important opportunities for businesses in an environment of slowing global growth. At the same time, greater innovation and investment from business is essential to meet Africa’s unfulfilled demand for goods and services, close the gaps in its infrastructure, create jobs, and decrease poverty.

    TCCIE is here to offer you all the support you need in your attempt to invest at this blessed continent with an abundance of resources in key sectors and help you translate the opportunities into profitable and sustainable enterprises.
