Investing in Ireland and Europe

Why should one consider investing in Ireland ?

Ireland is an attractive country to invest in. This is evidenced by the presence of a variety of foreign companies in the country from large established multinationals to high-growth scaling companies. There are currently thousands of international businesses that chose Ireland over other European countries, for simple reasons; Ireland offers the perfect ingredients that allows companies to grow quickly, and it has a great Education system that produces good talent.

Dublin for example is a vibrant, multicultural and creative city, it offers great quality of life and everyone is valued and welcomed here.

Foreign companies that have invested in Ireland are in sectors such as Real estate, Engineering , MedTech, Biopharma, Technology, Financial Services,Business Services, Media and others.

Real Estate in Ireland

Why should you invest in real estate in Ireland ?

  • A great plan for residency in Ireland and any other EU state because Ireland is part of the European union.
  • Ireland has a strong real estate market hence a guaranteed great return for your investment
  • Investment in real estate in a strong economy like Ireland means you stand to make a good profit for your investment whether you decide to rent out your property or sell it on.
