Why Choose Us?

We are an award winning Tanzania Chamber of Commerce Europe headquartered in Dublin, Rep of Ireland. We are the bridge that connects Tanzania/Africa with Ireland/Europe & the rest of the world .
We do the followings:

  • Agriculture (Growing, Buying & selling)
  • Investments
  • New Markets
  • Trade Facilitation
  • Business registration
  • Tourism
  • Project management )
  • Funding
  • Marketing & Social Media support
  • Events management
  • International study tours & exposure
  • Volunteering & Internships
